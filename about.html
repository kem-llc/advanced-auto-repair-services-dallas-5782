Our story

Dallas’s Trusted Auto Repair Team

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe and moving forward.

Advanced Auto Repair Services has been serving the Dallas community for over 25 years. Founded by master technician John Ramirez, our shop started as a small two-bay garage and has grown into a full-service facility thanks to our commitment to quality and integrity. We pride ourselves on treating every customer like a neighbor, offering transparent advice and fair pricing.

Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians handles everything from routine maintenance to complex engine repairs. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and ongoing training to ensure we can service modern vehicles effectively. Whether you drive a sedan, SUV, or truck, you can trust us to get the job done right the first time.
