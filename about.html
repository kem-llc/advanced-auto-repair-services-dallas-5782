Our story

Your Trusted Auto Repair Partner in Dallas Since 1995

Our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our customers safe and their vehicles running reliably. We believe in transparency, integrity, and building lasting relationships with every client.

Advanced Auto Repair Services was founded in 1995 by master mechanic John Smith with a simple vision: create a repair shop where customers are treated like family. Starting with just two bays in a small garage, John's commitment to quality work and fair prices quickly earned a loyal following. Over the years, we've grown to a full-service facility serving thousands of Dallas drivers.

Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians continues John's legacy of excellence. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and ongoing training to ensure we can handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs. We're proud to be a trusted part of the Dallas community and look forward to serving you.
